1. Who we are

This service ("Mu AI", "we", "us", "our") is operated by Ornella Kuate Konga, trading as Mu AI, a business registered in France under SIREN 837 927 961.

Registered address: 119 Boulevard Brune, 75014 Paris, France.

For any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at privacy@getmu.co.

Mu AI is a tool used by independent coaches and small businesses to manage and respond to direct message conversations with their own leads and customers on platforms such as Instagram. When a business uses Mu AI, we process the messages exchanged between that business and the people who contact it, so the business can respond. We act as a data processor on behalf of the business for those conversations, and as a data controller for our own customer and account records.

2. What information we collect

From people who message a business using Mu AI

  • Your social platform user ID and username
  • Your profile name and profile picture, where these are publicly available
  • The content of the messages you send to and receive from the business
  • Timestamps and basic metadata about those messages

We collect this only for conversations with businesses that use our service. We do not access your account beyond the conversation you have with the connected business.

From the businesses that use Mu AI (our customers)

  • Name, email address, and company name
  • Account and login information
  • Billing information, where applicable, processed through our payment provider; we do not store full payment card numbers ourselves

3. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Generate and suggest replies to messages, using artificial intelligence
  • Maintain conversation history so the business can respond in context
  • Route messages between the messaging platform and the business
  • Provide analytics to the business about response performance and engagement
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the service
  • Communicate with our business customers about their account
We do not sell your personal data. We do not use the content of conversations to train third-party AI models, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Legal basis for processing

Where the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we rely on the following legal bases: performance of a contract (to provide the service to our business customers), our legitimate interests and those of the businesses using Mu AI (to respond to and manage their own customer conversations), and consent where required. You can withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on it.

6. International data transfers

Mu AI is operated from France (the EU). Some of our service providers are located outside the EU, including in the United States. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards, such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses, to protect that data in line with the GDPR.

7. How long we keep your data

We keep conversation data and account information for as long as the business remains an active customer, and we delete or anonymise it within 24 months after a conversation ends or the account is closed, unless we are required to keep it longer by law. When data is no longer needed, or on a valid deletion request, we delete or anonymise it.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to erasure")
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@getmu.co. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. Because Mu AI processes messages on behalf of the business you contacted, some requests may also need to be directed to that business, and we will help coordinate where appropriate.

If you are in the EU and believe we have not handled your data properly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In France, this is the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertes).

9. How we protect your data

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit, access controls, and reputable infrastructure providers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data.

10. Children's privacy

Mu AI is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a child's data has been collected, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. The current version is always available at this page.
